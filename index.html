What Mythology Meant

Mythology is a way to explain how the universe was created and the law that the universe follows or phenomenons that occur in the world. Also some myth have an explanation of the world ending and brining a new generation of either gods, creatures, and humans, basically new life.

Symbolism

Mythologies bring many symbolism that artist, authors, politics, games, and more that can use symbols in mythology to use in thair creation to give a deeper meaning, some information of what's to come, or simething else.
